ABOUT

The lack of general awareness about deaf issues, and in particular the lack of sign language users in every day public spaces create the biggest barriers to deaf youths integration into society. Our project is aimed at helping people connect better with each other. We have developed a way for people with hearing impartment to be able to communicate with those who do not know their language. Using this program, user can sign multiple words with one gesture and copy the translated text with the click of a button.

When we speak of language barrier we subconsciously think of spoken languages but what about the language that can not be heard? Our project focuses on translation of sign language forÂ better integration of deaf youths into society. Most of the technology meant to help the deaf focused on converting speech to text. But very few actually focused on their language. So we came up with the idea of converting sign language to text. It will not only help people communicate but also foster an interest in the laguage.

In future we'll try and implement a video call feature so that the deaf can easily talk with other people who do not know their sign language.
